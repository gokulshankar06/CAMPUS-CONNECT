Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top navigation bar and fill out the registration form with your details. You'll need to verify your email address before you can log in.

Browse the events page to find an event you're interested in. Click on the event to view its details, then click the "Register" button to sign up. Some events may require approval from the event organizer.

To organize an event, you'll need to have an event manager account. Once logged in, go to your dashboard and click on "Create New Event." Fill out the event details, set the date and time, and submit for approval if required by your institution.

Click on your username in the top navigation bar and select "Profile" from the dropdown menu. On your profile page, you can edit your personal information, change your profile picture, and update your password.

On the login page, click on the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

Still have questions?

If you can't find the answer to your question in our FAQ, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. We're here to help!
